RegrooveFX

RegrooveFX is a collection of audio effects, synthesizers and tracker replayers available as VST/LV2 plugins, Logue units, and Rack modules.

  • Tracker replayers for .mod, .med, and .ahx formats
  • Essential effects: delay, reverb, EQ
  • Model 1 input processing: contour, sculpt
  • Drum engine based on AHX synthesis
  • Virtual analog reproduction of the x0x synth and drums

Packaged and downloadable versions of these effects are available on Itch.

  • Logue units for NTS1-MK2, NTS3, DrumLogue and MicroKorg 2
  • Rack modules for VCV Rack
